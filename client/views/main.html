Help us create a contemporary-sound-culture for each village/city/town. Let everyone discover the poetry of your favourite place, create your own soundtrack and subscribe in the app to join and share!

1

REACH YOUR SPOT

Where do you go when angry, to take a stroll and steam off a bit? Where do you daydream when in love or simply serene? Choose your place in the city, the one you find poetic and worth it . Think about how to express the feelings this spot arise inside you and when exactly during the day you like it the most!

3

ENRICH IT WITH YOUR MUSIC

Grab your craziest friend (otherwise find a brand-new one) and start mixing stuff on a music program to add soul to your basic-ambient recording and create your tune! Try to convey your sensation in the track, personalization is the way

2

RECORD ITS SOUND

Take you phone/recorder/zoom and go out to record this ambient sound which will be the basis of your track! Explore the surroundings, daytime, nightime, be brave! Record the moment you feel right, no matter if crowd is shouting, or the city is quiet. Pick the best and keep it, listen to it and get inspiration
